About GAK

A Legacy of Leadership

Garrison Academy Kharian stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the region, built on a strong foundation of military discipline, academic rigor, and character development. For over four decades, we have been shaping the minds of tomorrow's leaders.

Our institution operates under the esteemed APSACS framework, ensuring standardized excellence across all facets of education. We combine traditional values with modern pedagogical approaches to create well-rounded individuals ready to serve the nation.

With a dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a commitment to holistic development, GAK remains committed to producing not just academically brilliant students, but responsible citizens with strong moral values and leadership qualities.
